        <p>The Southfield Public School District is comprised of notable schools for every grade level.         <p>The best part of Southfield is the community. This city has the best of both worlds: a big city with a small town feel. Neighbors help each other, check on each other, and share goodies. The community as a whole cares about what happens in Southfield and do their best to treat it right.</p>
